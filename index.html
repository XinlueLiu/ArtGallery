The Dream

For many years, painting waited quietly.

Life became busy. Work came first. Family came first. Time moved on.

At sixty years old, I picked up a brush again.

My hope is simple: to continue painting, to share my work, to teach those who wish to learn, and one day to hold an exhibition somewhere in the world.
